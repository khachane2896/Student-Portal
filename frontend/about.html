Features

An online student portal designed for study can be a dynamic, all-in-one platform that fosters learning, collaboration, and personal development.

Personalized Dashboard

A centralized location where students can quickly view their upcoming assignments, grades, class schedules, and important announcements.

Course Management

Easy access to syllabus, lecture notes, reading materials, and video lectures. Integration of multimedia such as quizzes, video tutorials, discussion forums, and interactive exercises to enhance engagement. A visual representation of the student’s progress in each course, including completed assignments, exam scores, and grades.

Assignments & Exams

A simple and secure place for submitting assignments, projects, and essays. Transparent grading system where students can view their results, feedback from instructors, and areas for improvement. Notifications for upcoming assignments and deadlines.
